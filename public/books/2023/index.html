Books Read in 2023

These are the books I read in 2023, a mix of technical books, science fiction, and memoirs.

The Pragmatic Programmer

By David Thomas & Andrew Hunt
Finished: February 1, 2023 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5)
A must-read for software developers at any level, full of practical wisdom.

Dune

By Frank Herbert
Finished: March 15, 2023 ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)
Epic science fiction with incredible world-building that stands the test of time.

The Design of Everyday Things

By Don Norman
Finished: May 20, 2023 ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4)
Changed how I see product design and user experience in everyday objects.

The Three-Body Problem

By Cixin Liu
Finished: July 10, 2023 ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)
Fascinating hard sci-fi with unique perspective and mind-bending concepts.

Educated

By Tara Westover
Finished: September 5, 2023 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5)
Powerful memoir about self-invention, family, and the pursuit of knowledge.
